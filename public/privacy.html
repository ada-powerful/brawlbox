BrawlBox Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

BrawlBox ("we", "the app") is a browser-based fighting-game character creator at brawlbox.gg. This policy explains what we collect and why.

Information we collect

  • Account info. When you sign in with email, Google, or Facebook, we receive your email address and name to create and identify your account. We never receive or store your social-login password.
  • Content you create. Characters and sprites you generate, which you may save to your account.
  • Prompts. The text descriptions you submit are sent to our AI providers (OpenAI, fal.ai) to generate characters and sprites.

How we use it

To authenticate you, generate and store your characters, and operate and improve the service. We do not sell your personal information.

Third parties

We use Amazon Web Services (hosting, auth, storage), Cloudflare (delivery), and AI providers (OpenAI, fal.ai) strictly to provide the service.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time — see our data deletion instructions.
